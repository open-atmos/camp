\doxysubsubsection{CAMP\+: Special CMAQ Reaction for OH+\+HNO3}
\hypertarget{camp_rxn__c_m_a_q__o_h__h_n_o3}{}\label{camp_rxn__c_m_a_q__o_h__h_n_o3}\index{CAMP: Special CMAQ Reaction for OH+HNO3@{CAMP: Special CMAQ Reaction for OH+HNO3}}
For the reaction\+:

\[\ce{
      OH + HNO3 ->[k] NO3 + H2O
    }\]

CMAQ rate constants are calculated as\+:

\[
      k=k_0+(\frac{k_3[\mbox{M}]}{1+k_3[\mbox{M}]/k_2})
    \]

where $k_0$, $k_2$ and $k_3$ are \doxylink{camp_rxn_arrhenius}{Arrhenius} rate constants with $D=300$ and $E=0$, and $[\mbox{M}]$ is the concentration of air {\bfseries [Gipson]}.

 The key-\/value pairs {\bfseries{reactants}}, and {\bfseries{products}} are required. Reactants without a {\bfseries{qty}} value are assumed to appear once in the reaction equation. Products without a specified {\bfseries{yield}} are assumed to have a {\bfseries{yield}} of 1.\+0.

The three sets of parameters beginning with {\bfseries{k0\+\_\+}}, {\bfseries{k2\+\_\+}}, and {\bfseries{k3\+\_\+}}, are the \doxylink{camp_rxn_arrhenius}{Arrhenius} parameters for the $k_0$, $k_2$ and $k_3$ rate constants, respectively. When not present, {\bfseries{\+\_\+A}} parameters are assumed to be 1.\+0, {\bfseries{\+\_\+B}} to be 0.\+0, and {\bfseries{\+\_\+C}} to be 0.\+0.

The unit for time is assumed to be s, but inclusion of the optional key-\/value pair {\bfseries{time}} {\bfseries{unit}} = {\bfseries{MIN}} can be used to indicate a rate with min as the time unit.
