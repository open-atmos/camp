\doxysubsubsection{CAMP\+: Special CMAQ Reaction for H2\+O2}
\hypertarget{camp_rxn__c_m_a_q__h2_o2}{}\label{camp_rxn__c_m_a_q__h2_o2}\index{CAMP: Special CMAQ Reaction for H2O2@{CAMP: Special CMAQ Reaction for H2O2}}
For the reactions\+:

\[\ce{
   HO2 + HO2 ->[k] H2O2
 }\]  \[\ce{
   HO2 + HO2 + H2O ->[k] H2O2
 }\]

CMAQ rate constants are calculated as\+:

\[
   k=k_1+k_2[\mbox{M}]
 \]

where $k_1$ and $k_2$ are \doxylink{camp_rxn_arrhenius}{Arrhenius} rate constants with $D=300$ and $E=0$, and $[\mbox{M}]$ is the concentration of air {\bfseries [Gipson]}.

 The key-\/value pairs {\bfseries{reactants}}, and {\bfseries{products}} are required. Reactants without a {\bfseries{qty}} value are assumed to appear once in the reaction equation. Products without a specified {\bfseries{yield}} are assumed to have a {\bfseries{yield}} of 1.\+0.

The two sets of parameters beginning with {\bfseries{k1\+\_\+}} and {\bfseries{k2\+\_\+}} are the \doxylink{camp_rxn_arrhenius}{Arrhenius} parameters for the $k_1$ and $k_2$ rate constants, respectively. When not present, {\bfseries{\+\_\+A}} parameters are assumed to be 1.\+0, {\bfseries{\+\_\+B}} to be 0.\+0, and {\bfseries{\+\_\+C}} to be 0.\+0.

The unit for time is assumed to be s, but inclusion of the optional key-\/value pair {\bfseries{time}} {\bfseries{unit}} = {\bfseries{MIN}} can be used to indicate a rate with min as the time unit.
